Pudding recipe

Description

Caramel Bread Pudding Recipe (Without Oven) - Steamed Caramel Bread Pudding with Step wise pictures. Caramel Bread Pudding Recipe. Delicious and such a easy pudding which taste so yummy. It is one of the best desert you can make with bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread Slices - 2 big slices around 1 cup cubed
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 2 tblsp
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk - ¼ cup
  • Eggs - 2
  • Vanilla Essence - 2 tsp
  • Sugar - ¼ cup for caramel
  • Ghee - 1 tsp

How to make pudding

  1. Take bread, milk, sugar, condensed milk, eggs and vanilla in a blender and puree till smooth.
  2. Take a baking pan, grease with some ghee. Sprinkle some sugar over it and place it over heat.
  3. Now cool the tin for few mins.
  4. Pour the prepared custard on top.
  5. Cover the pan with foil or plastic wrap and place it in a steamer and cook for 40 to 45 mins.
  6. Remove it and insert a knife to check whether the cake is done or not.
  7. Now pop the pan in fridge and let it chill for 2 hours.
  8. Slice and serve.
